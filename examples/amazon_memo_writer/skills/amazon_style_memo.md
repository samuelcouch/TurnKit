---
name: Amazon Style Memo
description: Research, draft, and edit strict Amazon-style memos for event organizer and photographer audiences.
---

Workflow:

1. Use web_search first to find source candidates.
2. Use read_web_page for at least two sources before citing them.
3. Draft from evidence only. Keep numerical claims, statistics, and comparisons
   tied to a source.
4. Frame the customer problem around practical event work: registration, check-in,
   photo discovery, staff workload, attendee questions, sponsor value, and photo
   delivery.
5. Write for non-technical event organizers and photographers. Define or simplify
   technical terms before using them.
6. Use a professional, approachable, helpful, educational tone. Be confident but
   not salesy.
7. Replace buzzwords with concrete descriptions of what the feature does for the
   event team or photographer.
8. Edit paragraphs down to short blocks with clear whitespace.
9. Add a short transition sentence when a section changes topic abruptly.
10. Rank every list from most important to least important.
11. Remove Unicode em dash and en dash characters. Use periods, commas,
    parentheses, or "which" and "that" clauses instead.
12. Finalize by calling submit_amazon_memo with structured fields. Do not output
    free-form final Markdown yourself.
